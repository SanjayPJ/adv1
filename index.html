About the event

Advyka is the annual techno-cultural fest of College of Engineering Perumon, which started off in 2018. Advyka provides a platform for all to experience one of the most beautiful blend of science and technology with pure delight and enthusiasm.With a display of various events, competitions & workshops,

Advyka is an ideal destination for the students to learn and create.It offers an arena for students to compete,excel and prove themselves both technically and artistically.The students joined hands last year & went on to witness the beginning of something that is definitely bound to last a very long time.
